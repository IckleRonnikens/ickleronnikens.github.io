Hermione Granger

(Klaus Baudelaire)

Hermione Granger is the second orphan aquired by Lord Voldemort and one of the main protagonists of Harry Potter and the Series of Unfortunate Events. She is slightly younger than fellow orphan, Harry Potter, and quite older than Teddy Lupin, and is the only girl among the group.

She is very intelligent and enjoys reading books and conducting research for fun. However, she is also an extremely unlucky and unfortunate girl.

Hermione is fourteen at the beginning of the series, turns fifteen while incarcerated for a brief period of time in The Grim Gretto, and is sixteen when she leaves the Island at the end of The End.

Despite his research, IckleRonnikens does not know what happened to Hermione after the final novel. There is a possibility that Hermione and her fellow orphans died, either while leaving the island in The End or during other untrackable circumstances.
