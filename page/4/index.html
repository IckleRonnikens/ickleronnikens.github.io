About HMS Harmony

Welcome To Canon, Bitches!

Here at HMS Harmony, we go pretty hard when it comes to shipping the pairing of Harry and Hermione from the very popular Potter franchise, and that's only the beginning. As you will soon discover, our enthusiasm for the rightful pairing of the franchise doesn't end with a stubborn conversation about what's canon and not, it pours out of us through the mediums of fanfiction, fanart, and even fan podcasts.

Perhaps some of us are guilty of going over the top - maybe just a little bit - but such is the passion of our community. Who doesn't love a little bit of over-the-top enthusiasm when it comes to creating content for the fandom and pairing that your heart belongs too?

So, whether you're with us, against us, or just a little curious, dive in with us as we explore some pretty cool Harmony stuff.

HMS Harmony Blog

Sometimes, it's good to let loose, forgo all sense of reason, and write like there's no tomorrow. At HMS Harmony, all of us are guilty of doing such a thing, especially when it comes to activities in the community. Because who doesn't love a little bit of friendly compteition to see who can rack up the biggest word counts or asmass the most artworks?
